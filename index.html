Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for an meal. This classic omellette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetable or meats.

Preparation

  • Total: Approximately 10 mins
  • Preparation: 5 mins
  • Cooking: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs
